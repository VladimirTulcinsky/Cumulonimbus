In a mixing bowl, cream together 100g of unsalted butter and 100g of sugar until light and fluffy.
Beat in 2 large eggs, one at a time, until fully incorporated.
Stir in 100g of almond flour and 1 tsp of vanilla extract.
Roll out puff pastry or shortcrust pastry and spread the Frangipane mixture evenly over it.
Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and the Frangipane is set.
